Action Prioritization Guide

Recommended execution order

The strongest results usually come from fixing brand absence first, then improving content and entity clarity, and finally strengthening schema and support signals.

Step 1
Fix brand absence first

Queries where the brand is not mentioned are often the fastest path to stronger visibility wins.

Step 2
Improve weak content and entity clarity

Strengthen homepage, service pages, about page, and FAQs so AI systems can identify who you are and what you offer.

Step 3
Add structured support signals

Schema, trust elements, proof, and direct-answer formatting can make content easier to surface and quote.
