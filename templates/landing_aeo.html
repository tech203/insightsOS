Why AEO matters now

Search behaviour changed. Most companies still measure the wrong thing.

FAQ

Questions buyers will ask

What is the difference between SEO and AEO?

SEO focuses on ranking pages in search results. AEO focuses on improving the chances that your brand appears inside AI-generated answers and recommendations.

How do you measure AEO progress?

We use query-level tests, brand mention tracking, comparison snapshots, content coverage reviews, and implementation progress—not rankings alone.

What is the difference between the audit, the brief, and the draft?

The audit shows where you are visible and where you are being missed. The brief plans what content should be created. The draft is the first written version of that content.

Do I need new content?

In many cases, yes. Common gaps include service explainers, FAQs, comparison pages, and proof-rich content that is easier for answer engines to understand and reuse.

Is this for self-serve users or for agencies?

Both. Self-serve users can run the workflow themselves, while agencies can manage multiple clients and optionally use the white-label add-on for delivery.

Is this a guarantee that AI tools will recommend my brand?

No. It is a structured system for identifying gaps, improving content and brand clarity, and increasing your readiness for answer engines over time.
