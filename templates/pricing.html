Common Questions

Clear answers so pricing feels easier to understand.

Do plans and credits mean the same thing?

No. Plans define your workspace access and included monthly credit bundle. Credits are consumed when you run key actions.

Can I buy extra credits without upgrading?

Yes. Credit packs let you keep your current plan while increasing usage capacity.

Do I need the Agency add-on?

Only if you need white-label, branded, client-facing delivery. Most self-serve users do not need it.

Which plan should a small business choose?

Most small businesses should start with Pro, then move to Growth only if they need more frequent usage.

Start small, then scale when needed
